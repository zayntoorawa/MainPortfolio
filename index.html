About

Analyst by training.
Problem-solver by instinct.

At Bentley Motors, management would point me at a department with a broken process and say: figure it out. No brief, no PM, no BA. I'd go in, talk to people, map what was broken, gather evidence, and deliver something that fixed it.

That experience shaped how I work. I ask questions before proposing solutions. I challenge assumptions with data. I translate complex business and user needs into clear, actionable requirements — and I own the outcome, not just the deliverable.

Now at BAE Systems Digital Intelligence, I work on enterprise HR platforms — collaborating with vendors, gathering user feedback, managing risk and compliance, and delivering iterative improvements in an Agile environment.
